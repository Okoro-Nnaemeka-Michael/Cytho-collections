Care Tips for Bone Straight Hair

To maintain the luxurious texture and longevity of your bone straight hair, follow these simple care tips:

  • Apply a heat protectant when using heat styling tools to prevent damage.
  • Use sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners to maintain its shine and softness.
  • Bone straight hair retains its sleekness best when not overexposed to water.
  • When not in use, store the hair on a mannequin head or a silk bag to avoid tangles.

How to care for your bounce hair

Wash Regularly

Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to maintain its shine and softness.

Deep Wave Hair

Your ultimate choice for bold and elegant hairstyles

Deep wave hair is a luxurious and highly versatile hair texture that offers a naturally curly and voluminous look. It's a favorite among hair enthusiasts for its striking appeal and versatility. Deep wave hair is characterized by its smooth, consistent waves that blend beautifully with natural hair. Its texture lies between curly and wavy, making it an ideal choice for individuals seeking a voluminous yet manageable style. It can be worn naturally or styled in various ways, including straightening or curling.

Deep wave hair is often sourced from countries like Brazil, Peru, and Malaysia. These regions are renowned for producing high-quality, virgin human hair with a natural bounce and shine. The hair is carefully processed to retain its texture and durability.

Care Tips For Deep Wave Hair?

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to preserve its texture.
  • Avoid excessive heat to maintain the natural waves.
  • Apply leave-in conditioner to keep it moisturized and prevent frizz.
  • Comb gently with a wide-tooth comb to detangle.
